## Overview
Copper carbonate is an important copper-based inorganic material widely used as a precursor in chemical synthesis, catalyst systems, and pigment applications. 

Our material is produced via **controlled precipitation processes**, ensuring stable composition and consistent performance across different industrial requirements.

---

## Production Process Options

### 1. Standard Grade (Wet Process)
A cost-efficient production route designed for general industrial applications where balanced performance and economy are required.
* **Suitable for:** Bulk chemical processing
* **Advantage:** Stable quality for standard applications and flexible specification control.

### 2. High Purity Grade (Ammonia Process)
A high-control synthesis route designed for applications requiring superior purity and precise material characteristics.
* **Superior Purity:** High copper content with very low heavy metal levels.
* **Precision:** Controlled particle structure.
* **Applications:** Ideal for catalyst and high-performance chemical systems.

## Application Areas
* **Catalyst precursors** for chemical synthesis.
* **Fine chemical intermediates**.
* **Pigments** and ceramic glaze systems.
